Community-led regeneration

Clean, practical solutions for stronger homes and stronger communities.

What BAFT stands for

Built around community needs, not generic templates.

Housing opportunity

Supporting fairer routes into housing

BAFT was shaped by research showing many young people in impoverished areas across Kirklees may struggle to access quality housing, affecting stability, pride and future life chances.

Community resource

Rebuilding shared spaces

The model also addresses the loss of vital local resources by exploring how old, underused or derelict buildings can become valuable community places again.

Collective action

Self-build as a social model

The common thread is people organising together to create the kind of homes, spaces and communities they want to see for themselves and future generations.
