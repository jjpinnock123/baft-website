How we work

A practical step-by-step model for community-led development.

BAFT’s process combines community buy-in, planning and delivery, so projects are rooted in local need and built with long-term viability in mind.

1

Develop a steering committee

BAFT works with team members and community members through engagement events to build local ownership, test whether a project is genuinely needed and explore viability through evidence and business planning.

2

Build a project proposal

The next stage focuses on site selection, funding planning and terms of reference, turning ideas into a more structured proposal that can guide delivery.

3

Deliver the project

This is the construction and implementation phase, where project management, construction activity and coordination become the core priority.

4

Complete and sustain

Opening and running the building or project is the final stage, supported by planning for people, systems, finance, fit-out, launch, monitoring and long-term impact.
